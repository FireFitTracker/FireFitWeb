Privacy Policy

Your data stays protected.

FireFit only collects the information required to power your account, logs, check-ins, progress history, and product improvements.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as name, email, and profile settings.
  • App usage data related to calorie logs, workout logs, progress photos, and check-ins.
  • Device and analytics data used to improve reliability, performance, and product quality.

How we use it

  • To provide core app features and keep your fitness records available across sessions.
  • To improve projections, user experience, and application stability.
  • To communicate support updates or respond to account requests.

How we protect it

  • We use secure systems and restricted access to protect account information.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • You can contact us at hello@firefit.app for help with your account data.
