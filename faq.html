FAQ

Common questions, answered clearly.

FireFit is built to make tracking and progress easy to understand. These are the most common questions people ask before downloading.

Who is FireFit for?

FireFit is for anyone who wants a disciplined, simple system for body recomposition, weight loss, and fitness consistency.

What can I track in the app?

You can track weight, calorie intake, calories burned, progress photos, workout plans, completed sets, and longer-term projections.

Does FireFit show future progress?

Yes. Projection screens estimate pace, milestones, and goal timing based on the data you log consistently.

Can I log both food and activity?

Yes. FireFit includes calorie logging for food as well as activity logging so your net intake is easier to manage.

Does FireFit include workout planning?

Yes. You can build a workout plan by day, log sets and reps, and check off completed items as you train.

How do I get support?

Email hello@firefit.app and we will help with access, questions, or support issues.
